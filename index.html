Our Church

Welcome to St. George Orthodox Church. Our church serves the Indian Orthodox community in the London, Ontario area. The Holy Qurbana is celebrated every second and fourth Saturdays. The church emphasizes the importance of instilling Orthodox Christian values in its members. If you are looking for a church that is orthodox in doctrine, orthodox in worship, and orthodox in values, please join us on Saturdays.

St. George Indian Orthodox Church is part of the Malankara Orthodox Church (also known as Indian Orthodox Church) which is founded by St. Thomas, one of the twelve apostles of Jesus Christ who came to India in AD 52.

Our church comes under the Northeast American diocese, which is governed by His Grace Zachariah mar Nicholovos.
